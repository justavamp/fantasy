The World Through the
Eyes of a Bookworm

For me, each story that I have read till date
has successfully taken me to a world where I could
easily imagine myself as a part of a world where
everything is different as seen from the real wolrd. Some
people might call this as an Escaping Mechanism, but this
had eventually become a part of me. A part that
I still wish existed within me.
